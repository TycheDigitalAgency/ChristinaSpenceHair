My Story

When you feel good, you look good.

Hola beautiful, welcome to my business! I'm Christina Spence, originally from California and now proudly serving our Las Vegas, Nevada community.

As a woman I know exactly how important it is to feel special, pampered and beautiful — time is precious, and I have selected a beautiful, clean private space for you to feel relaxed, uplifted and more confident than ever!

I've been a hairstylist for over 17 years and I am so in love with my craft and my guests. I have a heart for making people feel comfortable. I specialize in natural looking hair extensions that blend seamlessly into your own hair. The method I offer is safe for women with thinning hair. I work closely with you to regain or maintain the health of your natural hair. I understand the struggle and concern of thinning hair. My efforts are to always meet you where your unique needs are by customizing every step — color, placement, cut and reparative products that strengthen for shiny, healthy looking hair and extensions.

When I'm not in the salon I love to travel, visit coffee shops and dine with family and friends. I love health and wellness and all things natural beauty but still enjoy vegan donuts, sushi and chips & salsa. I love hanging out with my adult children every chance I get. They laugh at all my quirky jokes! I've been married to my husband since 2018 and we love to relax and watch live sports, especially soccer.

I am a Licensed Signature Hair Extension Artist.

My passion for creating confidence-enhancing looks for each client is evident in my attention to detail and commitment to providing the highest level of service. I have cultivated a loyal following of discerning clientele, including many affluent women who seek out my expertise for their signature looks.
